The second version of corona as an HMPV virus

Human Metapneumovirus (HMPV) is a contagious respiratory virus affecting people of all ages. Symptoms include cough, fever, runny nose, and shortness of breath. In severe cases, HMPV can lead to pneumonia, bronchiolitis, and hospitalization. Transmission occurs through close contact, contaminated surfaces, and airborne droplets. Treatment focuses on relieving symptoms, with antiviral medications available for high-risk individuals..
